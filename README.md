<h1 align="center">Cancer Data Analysis Project</h1>

<p>
This project focuses on analyzing cancer-related datasets to identify patterns, trends, and insights about cancer incidence and mortality. 
The goal is to explore multiple datasets and understand the most common cancer types, their distribution, and possible factors associated with cancer cases.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📊 Project Objectives</h2>

<ul>
<li>Analyze cancer incidence and mortality trends.</li>
<li>Identify the most common cancer types.</li>
<li>Study cancer statistics in Egypt and globally.</li>
<li>Explore relationships between cancer cases and other indicators.</li>
<li>Create visual dashboards to present insights.</li>
</ul>
